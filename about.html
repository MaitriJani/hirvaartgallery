About Hirva

Darshil's fullname is Darshil Dipak Jani. He studies in St. Xavier's CMI Public School located in Bhavnagar. Currently he is in 8th grade. He loves to paint different art forms and play video games. His mother's name is Jagruti .D. Jani, a kind hearted woman. He has an elder sister, named Maitri. Darshil's favorite colors are black and blue.
